FGM Scoring Options

1. Round Scoring (total strokes +/- par):
Example:
Round 1: -3
Round 2: -4
Round 3: -3
Round 4: -1
Total: -11

Missed Cut Penalty:
A. Identify the **10 worst players who made the cut** (highest overall scores).
B. Calculate their **average score to par for each round** (be sure to round, no decimals).
C. Compare that average to a **minimum penalty of +4**.
D. Use whichever value is **greater**.
E. Do this after round 3 and round 4. 

2. Hole Scoring:
Eagle (or better): +7pts
Birdie: +3pts
Par: +.5pts
Bogey: -1pt
Double Bogey (or worse): -3pts
